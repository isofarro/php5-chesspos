ChessPos: Chess position image creator

Creates an image of a chessboard specified by FEN. Given the FEN string:

6k1/3q2p1/p2bp2p/3p1r2/1p1Pp3/3bQ1PP/PP1B1rB1/1N2R1RK

and changing / to -, the URL of the image is:

http://chesspos.myapps.info/board/6k1-3q2p1-p2bp2p-3p1r2-1p1Pp3-3bQ1PP-PP1B1rB1-1N2R1RK.gif
